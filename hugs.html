Health Uganda Group Sheffield (HUGS)

HUGS is a group of Ugandan people living in Sheffield who support the work of the Sheffield Health International Partnerships (SHIP).

It has three main aims:
  • To advise SHIP in its work and be a point of consultation for the development of its plans
  • To support fund raising for the work of SHIP
  • To support Ugandan mental health professionals when they visit the UK as part of the Sheffield- Gulu Mental Health Partnerships and other links.

The group meets approximately bi-monthly and for more details please contact the Chair Rodrigo Edema.
