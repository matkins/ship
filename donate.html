The Sheffield-Gulu Partnership is working in Uganda and the UK to improve the support available to people with mental health problems.

This involves the Mental Health Ward at Gulu Regional Referral Hospital, the Gulu branch of Mental Health Uganda and Sheffield Health and Social Care.

SHIP is a registered charity that combines two international health links working from Sheffield into Uganda. These are the Sheffield-Gulu Mental Health Partnership and the Sheffield Medical Group.
